Our Return Policy

To be eligible for a return or exchange, your item must be in the same condition that you received it, unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging. You'll also need the receipt or proof of purchase.

Eligible for Return

  • Items within 30 days of delivery
  • Unworn, unused with original tags
  • In original packaging

Not Eligible

  • Items past 30 days
  • Worn or used items
  • Custom or personalized items

How to Return

1

Initiate Your Return

Visit our returns portal and enter your order number and email address to start your return.

2

Pack Your Item

Place the item in its original packaging with all tags attached. Include the return slip in your package.

3

Ship It Back

Attach the prepaid shipping label we provide and drop off at any carrier location.

4

Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5 business days.
