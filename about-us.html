Our Story

From dorm room sketches to premium jerseys worn worldwide

AJ Mensah

Founder & Creative Director

"What began as a university side hustle is now a brand that connects athletes and fans across continents."

The Solo Grind

In 2018, while studying at university, I started designing custom jerseys for campus teams. With just a laptop, a vision, and relentless hustle, I turned my passion into a business.

Late nights turned into early mornings as I balanced classes with fulfilling orders. The demand grew beyond campus, and soon I was shipping jerseys nationwide.

In 2023, three visionary investors saw the potential in AJ Globals and helped transform it from a one-man operation into the premium brand it is today.

Our Journey

1

2018 - The Beginning

Started designing custom jerseys for university teams while studying full-time. First 50 orders fulfilled from a dorm room.

2

2020 - First Website

Launched our first e-commerce site, expanding beyond campus to serve customers nationwide.

3

2022 - Premium Materials

Partnered with international manufacturers to source premium fabrics and introduce limited edition drops.

4

2023 - Investor Partnership

Secured investments from three strategic partners to scale operations and expand our global reach.

5

Today - Global Presence

Shipping premium jerseys to over 15 countries with a growing community of athletes and fans.
