How to Measure

Chest/Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest/bust, keeping the tape measure horizontal and snug but not tight.

Length

Measure from the highest point of the shoulder down to the bottom hem of the jersey.

Sleeve

Measure from the shoulder seam to the end of the sleeve (for short sleeves) or wrist (for long sleeves).

Fit Guide

Standard Fit

Our standard fit jerseys are designed for comfort and mobility. They have a relaxed silhouette that allows for full range of motion.

  • Roomier in chest and shoulders
  • Straight cut through torso
  • Standard sleeve length

Athletic Fit

Our athletic fit jerseys are tailored for performance with a more contoured silhouette that follows the body's natural shape.

  • Tapered through torso
  • Extended shoulder articulation
  • Shorter hem for mobility

Unsure About Your Size?

Our customer service team is happy to help you find your perfect fit. Contact us with your measurements and we'll recommend the best size for you.
