Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our premium sports jerseys, ordering process, shipping, and more.

Our jerseys are crafted with premium materials, featuring exclusive designs that blend global style with sports heritage. Each piece undergoes rigorous quality control and is produced in limited quantities to ensure exclusivity. We use advanced moisture-wicking fabrics and reinforced stitching for durability.

We provide detailed size charts for each product. Measure your chest, waist, and hips, then compare with our measurements. Our jerseys have an athletic fit - if you prefer a looser fit, consider sizing up.

For personalized sizing advice, contact our customer service team with your measurements and we'll recommend the best size for you.

We accept returns and exchanges within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, with all original tags attached. Final sale items (marked as such) cannot be returned or exchanged.

To initiate a return, visit our Returns Center in your account or contact our customer service team. Return shipping is the customer's responsibility unless the item arrived damaged or defective.

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the US, 5-10 business days internationally. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.

During high-demand periods (new drops, holidays), processing may take an additional 1-2 business days. You'll receive tracking information as soon as your order ships.

Yes! We offer limited customization options for our premium jerseys. You can add your name, preferred number, or select from our curated customization options.

Custom orders take 7-10 additional business days to process and cannot be returned unless defective. Visit our Custom Jersey Builder or contact us for special requests.

To maintain your jersey's premium quality:

  • Machine wash cold with similar colors
  • Use mild detergent and avoid bleach
  • Turn inside out before washing
  • Hang dry or tumble dry low
  • Iron on low heat if needed (avoid printed areas)

Our jerseys are inspired by global sports culture but are original AJ Globals designs. We collaborate with artists and designers worldwide to create unique pieces that celebrate sports heritage without infringing on official team branding.

We release limited-edition drops quarterly, with occasional surprise releases throughout the year. Join our mailing list or follow us on social media @ajglobals to stay updated on upcoming releases and exclusive early access opportunities.
